Complete Application Consult

$7999

This service is designed to help you build your entire application from the ground up. We will walk you through each component of the application and help you decide what to include and how to present it. A Complete Application Consult covers all application components including:

  • Profile / Family / Education
  • Testing
  • Activities and Awards
  • Personal Essay
  • Supplemental Essays
  • Additional Information
  • Disciplinary Information

As the most full-service plan we offer, the Complete Application Consult includes:

  • Unlimited access via email for questions throughout the process
  • The Better College Apps Guides to Essays, Extracurricular Activities, Awards, Interviews, and Letters of Recommendation
  • The Better College Apps Introspection Worksheet
  • Resume and Information Worksheet
  • Email feedback and one hour planning session following worksheets submission
  • Fourteen additional hours of one-on-one consultation (15 hours total) to review and discuss essays and application materials. This also includes strategy, college choices, financial aid, and anything else for your college admissions process. These can be scheduled in 30 or 60 minute increments as needed. This will generally cover about eight full applications.

Since our goal is an outstanding end product, we will not accept a new Complete Application Consult within thirty days of an application deadline. We also require that all phone/video calls related to the application content be completed or scheduled by fifteen days before the deadline so there is sufficient time for revision. Audio recordings of all meetings will be provided.
